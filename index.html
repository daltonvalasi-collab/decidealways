Free random decision tools

DecideAlways is a collection of free online tools for random decisions and fair picks. Whether you need to flip a coin, spin a wheel, or generate a random number, every tool here is instant, accurate, and completely free.

Our tools are used by teachers picking students at random, streamers running live giveaways, families settling dinner debates, and anyone who wants a fast, fair, unbiased result without downloading an app or creating an account.

Unlike most online tools, DecideAlways uses crypto.getRandomValues() — the same cryptographic randomness used in browser security — instead of JavaScript's standard Math.random(), which can produce subtly biased results over many uses.
