Free online spin wheel

Add any choices to the wheel, click Spin, and get a random result. Great for picking a winner in a giveaway, deciding what to eat, selecting a student in class, or any decision you want to leave to chance.

Every spin uses crypto.getRandomValues() for genuine randomness — not the basic Math.random() that most tools use.

How do I add my own choices?

Type your entry in the text box on the right and press + or hit Enter. You can add up to 30 entries. To remove one, click the × next to it.

Is the spin truly random?

Yes — we use cryptographic randomness so every segment has exactly equal odds. There's no way to rig the result.

Can I use this for classroom name picking?

Absolutely. Add all your students' names and spin. You can remove a name after it's been picked to ensure everyone gets a turn.
