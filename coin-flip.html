About this coin flip tool

This free online coin flip uses crypto.getRandomValues() — the same cryptographically secure randomness your browser uses for security — to ensure every flip is genuinely 50/50. No patterns, no bias, no tricks.

Perfect for settling disputes, making quick decisions, classroom activities, game night, sports team selection, and anywhere you need a fair random result.

Is this coin flip truly random?

Yes. We use crypto.getRandomValues(), a cryptographically secure random number generator built into your browser. It's the same method used for SSL certificates and encryption. Every flip is independent — past results never influence future ones.

Can I flip multiple times quickly?

Yes — just click the button or tap the coin repeatedly. Each flip is instant and independent. Your full history and running totals are tracked on this page.
