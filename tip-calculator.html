Free tip calculator

Enter your bill total, select a tip percentage, and choose how many people are splitting the bill. The calculator instantly shows the tip amount and the total per person.

What tip percentage should I leave?

In the US, 15–20% is standard for sit-down restaurants. 10% is acceptable for counter service. In the UK, 10–12.5% is typical. In Australia and New Zealand, tipping is not expected but 10% is appreciated for excellent service.

How is the tip calculated?

Tip amount = Bill × (Tip% ÷ 100). Total per person = (Bill + Tip) ÷ Number of people. The calculator rounds to 2 decimal places.

Can I split unevenly?

This calculator assumes an even split. For uneven splits, calculate the total per person first, then adjust individual amounts manually.
