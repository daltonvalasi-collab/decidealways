DecideAlways is a collection of free online tools. We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal information to use any of our tools. The tools on this site (coin flip, spin wheel, random number generator, dice roller, timer, tip calculator, password generator, Magic 8 Ball, and food picker) all run entirely in your browser and do not transmit any user-generated data to our servers.
