The food decision wheel

We've all been there — standing in the kitchen or scrolling through delivery apps, completely unable to decide. This wheel fixes that. Spin it and commit to whatever it lands on.

Use the filter tabs to narrow by cuisine type, or add your own favourites in the custom mode. Remove anything you're not in the mood for and spin until you're hungry.

Can I add my own restaurants?

Yes — type anything in the "Add your own food" box and it gets added to the wheel immediately. Great for adding your local favourites or specific restaurants you're choosing between.

Advertisement

How do I remove a food I don't want?

Click the × next to any item in the list on the right to remove it from the wheel. The wheel updates instantly.

Can I use this for a group decision?

Absolutely — get everyone to agree on a shortlist, add them to the wheel, and spin. No more endless back-and-forth.
