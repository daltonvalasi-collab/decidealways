Free online dice roller

Roll one or two standard six-sided dice using cryptographic randomness. Every roll is completely independent — no patterns, no bias. Perfect for board games, tabletop RPGs, classrooms, and any game that needs a fair roll.

Is this truly random?

Yes. We use crypto.getRandomValues() for every roll — the same cryptographic randomness used in browser security. Each roll is genuinely independent.

Can I roll more than 2 dice?

The current tool supports 1 or 2 dice. For rolling multiple dice for D&D and other RPGs, each click gives you a fresh pair of results which you can use together.

What's the probability of rolling a 12 with 2 dice?

With two fair six-sided dice, rolling a 12 (double six) has a 1 in 36 chance — about 2.78%. Rolling a 7 is most likely at 6 in 36 (16.67%).
