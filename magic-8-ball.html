About the Magic 8 Ball

The original Magic 8 Ball was invented in 1950 and contains 20 responses — 10 positive, 5 neutral, and 5 negative. This digital version faithfully recreates all 20 classic answers with genuine randomness.

Ask any yes-or-no question, click the ball, and receive your mystical verdict. Great for decisions, parties, and anywhere you need a little cosmic guidance.

How many answers does it have?

Exactly 20 — the same as the original toy. Ten are positive ("It is certain", "Without a doubt"...), five are neutral ("Reply hazy, try again"...), and five are negative ("Don't count on it"...).

Is it truly random?

Yes. We use cryptographic randomness so every answer is genuinely unpredictable. No answer is weighted more than another.

Can I ask the same question twice?

Of course — the ball doesn't remember previous questions. Each shake is completely independent.
